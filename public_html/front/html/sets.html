At the close of the Age of Fire, all lands meet at the end of the earth.
Great kingdoms and anaemic townships will be one and the same.
The great tide of human enterprise, all for naught.
That's why I'm so taken by this grand sight. This must be what it's like to be a god.

An old cemetery with the purpose of stocking ashen bodies so that one day could be revived if anything goes wrong with the linking of the fire. It is unclear how undead came to be in the state of death, but from the sheer number of coffins it cannot be an uncommon fate.
Cementery of Ash
Many epochs ago Lordran also had a Firelink Shrine, a place that played a crucial role because it allowed the Chosen Undead to travel to the Kiln of the First Flame. It is hard to tell if this sanctuary is located in the same place as the old given the amount of time that has passed and how much has changed.
