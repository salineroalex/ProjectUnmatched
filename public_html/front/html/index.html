Unmatched - The Game

Welcome to the Unmatched fanatic's home page.

You can play as Sherlock Holmes and battle against Sinbad. Everything said and done.

Unmatched is a series of games which happen to build battles from 2 up to 4 characters, and offer a diverse set of fighters, cards, boards and sets. You can play as Alice from Alice in Wonderland and fight against Bruce Lee, Doctor Jekyll and Mr. Hyde or Sherlock Holmes, to say the least. There are differents types of sets, each one with unique heroes, boards and cards. Come and join us!

In this page you can find the aforementioned sets, cards, boards and characters. Navigate through every one and each of these elements to discover more about the world of Unmatched.

You just have to click on the tabs in the upper section of the page to begin discovering!

Have fun with Unmatched!
